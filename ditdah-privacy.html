Dit Dah — Privacy Policy

Dit Dah ("the app") is published by the Pixtory Development Team. This policy explains what the app does — and does not — do with your data. This policy applies to the Dit Dah Android application only.

What we collect

Nothing. Dit Dah does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app works fully offline. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers.


What is stored on your device

The app saves your learning progress — completed levels, XP, streak, and audio/haptic preferences — in your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app removes it.


Permissions

  • Camera (android.permission.CAMERA) — required by Android only so the app can turn on your phone's flashlight LED for visual Morse signaling. The app never opens the camera, captures images, or records video.
  • Vibrate (android.permission.VIBRATE) — used for haptic feedback when you tap a Morse key.
  • Internet (android.permission.INTERNET) — declared by the underlying framework but not used by the app for any data transfer.

Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.


Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
