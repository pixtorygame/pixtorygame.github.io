Letter Ring Puzzle



A Challenging Alphabet Adventure

Embark on a captivating journey through the alphabet with "Letter Ring Puzzle," a delightful and addictive game that combines strategic thinking with linguistic prowess. Prepare to immerse yourself in a world of letters, where every move counts and wordplay reigns supreme.

The objective of "Letter Ring Puzzle" is to manipulate the letters on a circular board, strategically arranging them, unlock the next letter in the sequence, and earn valuable points along the way. As you delve into the game, you'll discover a range of features that add depth and excitement to the experience.

About Game

A fun puzzle game based on the letters of the alphabet.

By tapping the space between the two letters around the circle, you can move the letter at the center of the circle to any place on the circle.

By placing a '+' between two similar letters, you can combine these letters and obtain the next letter in the alphabet.

When you catch a symmetry in the letter sequences on the circle, you can combine more letters with '+' at once, destroy more letters and earn more points.

The number of letters that can be placed on the circle is limited. Therefore, the game will end when there is no room left on the circle.

Before the circle is filled with letters, you should combine the letters and aim to constantly reduce the number of letters on the circle.
